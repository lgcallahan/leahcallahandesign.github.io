The Facing Project

Community Pro Bono Project / Character Illustration / Website Sliders / Animation

The Facing Project is a nonprofit that connects people through stories to strengthen communities. They provide tools, a platform, funds, and inspiration so communities can share the stories of citizens through the talent of local writers, artists, and actors.

Our client was in need of visual sliders as a part of their website to inform visitors of what The Facing Project is and does.

Working together as a team, we illustrator characters to depict the mission of The Facing Project in a friendly and understandable matter. Working in After Effects, we added animation to the illustrations to attract more attention to our slides and to keep the user engaged.
