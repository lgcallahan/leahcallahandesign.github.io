More About ME!

I am currently a Senior at Ball State University pursuing a BFA in Graphic Design.

I've learned to throw myself into any creative opportunity — you can find me in the studio at 2 am on the weekdays and 8 am on the weekends pouring every ounce of energy into my work. I've learned that as a designer there are times I fail, and hours of pushing through those failures and seeking criticism are how I achieve my best work.

My love for code has opened up a whole new door, and I have started to focus on a specialty of front-end web design.

When I'm not judging logos and comparing the UI of app updates, I enjoy Cincinnati Skyline Chili, soaking up sunshine in the great outdoors, and laughing. Please feel free to contact me! I am extremely eager to learn new things, meet new people, grow as a designer, and continue jumping into this creative industry!
