Frank Gehry

Architect Study / Informative Book

Designed and produced an information book about the famous work of architect, Frank Gehry. Researched post-modern and modern architects. Incorporated Gehry's constructivism style and materials into the design of the book, such as carboard.

Transformed his famous line sketches into transparent vinyl pages that are overlaid on top of the final buildings to show the connection of his thoughts to the final product.
