Enter Action

Art Exhibition Design / Campaign / Identity / Poster / Sketch Up

EnterAction is an exhibition designed to examine interactions; interaction with nature, technology, written language, the senses, oneself, and groups of people. In our daily lives, we find more and more people looking for an escape, trying to understand why they function the way that they do. Why is this? As we busy ourselves and find more and more distractions in life, we lose touch with the more nuanced aspects of our human nature. What is touch? What is language? What does it mean to interact with others? What is important about that interaction?

In our exhibition, we not only invite the viewer to interact, but to enter into action. To do something different with this new experience, to remind them to consider their world, others, and themselves as they participate in these subtle interactions daily. We are not seeking to show anything new, but to present what the viewer already intuitively knows in a more abrupt manner.
