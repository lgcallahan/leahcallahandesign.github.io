12 Month Laser Cut Calendar Redesign

I was challenged to create a 12-month calendar design for a class project. Each month focuses on a designer and their particular style of work. Researched their color palettes, use of typography, layout, typeface styles, and paid close attention to their work’s style.

Each quote associated with the month are the “Words of Wisdom” the designer has said. The designer’s name was laser cut into cardstock. Each day the user rips off a tab that displays the day’s date. As time progresses, and more tabs are ripped off, the quote underneath the first layer is slowly revealed. The contrast between the colored tabs and the background also visually depict the passing of time.
