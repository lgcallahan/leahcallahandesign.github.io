Design Between the Fold

Paper Promotion

I created a paper promotion to experiment with specialty printing techniques. The target audience was for other designers who would be receiving this promo in the mail from a paper company showing off their stock of paper available for purchase.

I wanted to give the user a challenge to solve and incorporate interaction into my piece. By folding the paper and looking at the pieces from a different perspective, the quote on each side becomes legible.
