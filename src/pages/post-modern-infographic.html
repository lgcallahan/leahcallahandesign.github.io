Post-Modern Designers

Infographic Poster

I was challenged to research and create an infographic about 30 post-modern designers and their connections to each other. From bottom to top diagonally depicts a timeline of the designers and the start of their career.

Connections include where they went to school, if they were an AIGA medalist, if they were part of the Punk style, where they were from, if they were an AGI member, and if they were a part of M&Co.
