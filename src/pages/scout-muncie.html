Scout Muncie

Community Pro Bono Infographic Poster

ScoutMuncie is a project led by the City of Muncie Historic Preservation and Rehabilitation Commission, and the Delaware County Historical Society to gather information on property conditions, occupancy rates, and potential historic structures.

ScoutMuncie was in need of presenting collected data to the community in an engaging and visual method. Collaborated with designer, Kathryn Holaday, to create an infographic poster for ScoutMuncie’s community engagement event.

Our work incorporates the phrase “What is Muncie’s Landscape?” because it displays the research of ScoutMuncie which describes the physical landscape of our city. Gathered data is integrated into the letterforms and surrounding the words, therefore answering the posed question. This poster illuminates complex research in a simplified, visual solution. Use of 3-dimensional typography symbolizes structures, parcels, and buildings, a key component that was studied in this research project. By applying a transparent style, this poster depicts Muncie having more depth, history, and promise than what is commonly known to the public.
