Intoxicated California Baby

Appropriation / Packaging / Identity

This project addresses the negative effects of alcohol consumption and binge drinking. I researched how frequently people, especially college students, do not drink responsibly. Therefore, others are usually forced to care for and provide for those who drink and cannot care for themselves.

This concept is similar to caring for a child or infant who cannot make mature choices on their own. By mixing alcohol labels with baby items, this project draws attention to the dangers of our irresponsible decisions that are ever so present in our American culture today.

The names of the labels were created by combining popular baby brand names. While the visual appearance is an appropriation of alcohol labels and packaging.
